About Me

Welcome! I am Thien An Nguyen, a self-taught guitarist with a passion that has grown over nearly a decade. My educational journey unfolded on platforms like YouTube, where I cultivated a deep mastery of the guitar. Based in the vibrant Bay Area, I am devoted to enriching our local music scene by sharing my love for music through teaching and performance.

My career has been marked by celebrated collaborations with notable Vietnamese artists such as Ho Ngoc Ha, Bui Anh Tuan, Vu Cat Tuong, Chi Dan, and Vo Ha Tram. These experiences have greatly enriched my musical diversity and allowed me to contribute meaningfully to the cultural tapestry of Vietnam and beyond.

Services Offered

Guitar Lessons

I offer personalized one-on-one guitar lessons tailored to fit the needs of each student. Whether you are in the Bay Area or prefer to learn online via Zoom, Discord, or another platform, my lessons provide flexible options to suit your learning style. Specializing in fingerstyle percussive guitar, I also offer lessons in composing Vietnamese songs and singing, with instruction available in both Vietnamese and English.

Live Performances

As a performer, I am eager to share my music at various events and venues. My performances have been recognized and celebrated across the United States, with first-place awards at talent shows in De Anza, San Jose City College, and the Tet Festival in San Jose. Highlights of my career include performing at the San Jose Museum of Arts and alongside Omar Alejandro in the Cantar Con Los Reyes program, reflecting my versatility and broad appeal.

In addition to public events, I am available for private gigs, where I bring my specialty in fingerstyle percussive guitar solos to create a memorable experience for all attendees.
