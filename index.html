About Me

Welcome! I'm Thien An Nguyen, a self-taught guitarist based in the vibrant Bay Area.

  • Background: I began my musical journey nearly a decade ago, using platforms like YouTube to develop a deep mastery of the guitar.
  • Teaching and Performing: I am passionate about enriching the local music scene through teaching and engaging live performances.
  • Collaborations: In the U.S., I have had the opportunity to work with many renowned Vietnamese singers from Thúy Nga and Asia entertainment centers. The artists I have collaborated with include Như Quỳnh, Trần Thái Hòa, Đan Nguyên, Dương Triệu Vũ, Don Hồ, Lâm Nhật Tiến, Lê Quốc Tuấn, Thiên Kim, etc. Additionally, I have worked with esteemed Vietnamese artists such as Hồ Ngọc Hà, Bùi Anh Tuấn, Vũ Cát Tường, Võ Hạ Trâm, and so on, contributing to international cultural exchanges and enriching musical diversity.
  • Awards: I am a recipient of several accolades in the United States, including first prizes at De Anza Got Talent, San Jose City College's Got Talent Show, and the Student Singing Contest at the Tet Festival in San Jose.
  • Notable Performances: I was showcased as a featured guitarist at the San Jose Museum of Arts, participated in the Cantar Con Los Reyes program alongside viral social media sensation Omar Alejandro, regularly played guitar in the American Dream Show by MAC Network featuring viral Vietnamese guests, and performed at the downtown San Jose art gallery and various other events.

I look forward to continuing to share my musical journey with you.
